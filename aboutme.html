About Me

I have been building websites and web applications since about 2001 when I was given the opportunity to step into the role with no previous experience. Through trail and error, online courses, and the great PHP/Web Dev community, I’ve been able to grow into the developer I am today.

Some past successes include working on high-profile sites such as Go Hawaii and Visit Dallas to large, custom, back-office solutions to manage all aspects of an ecommerce warehouse.

Outside of work, I’m a father of three great kids, a music fanatic, a craft beer enthusiast/evangelist. Played lock/flanker for the Fox Valley Rugby Football Club and hope to play again soon. Located in Sycamore, right in the middle of northern Illinois’ beautiful soybean and corn fields.
